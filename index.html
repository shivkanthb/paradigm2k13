SSN ACE & PARADIGM

Paradigm is an annual national technical symposium organized by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering.It is conducted by the students and the Association of Computer Science and Engineering(ACE) every year. As a jewel in the crown, Paradigm attracts several thousand students from hundreds of colleges and universities across India
