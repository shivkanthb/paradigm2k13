Rules for Dumb-C

  1. Only action ,no lip movement is allowed.

  2. Totally 2 rounds followed by finals.

  3. Each round is a elimination round.

  4. 1st round has 7 words from different fields(Time:2 mins).

  5. 2nd round has 5 Technical words(Time:1.30 mins).

  6. Finals will be a surprise round and it has many sub-rounds.

  7. Prize Money will be rewarded only to the top 3 Teams
